Hello! I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate in the Shanghai Institute for Advanced Immunochemical Studies (SIAIS) at ShanghaiTech University, advised by Prof. Fang Bai.

My research interests are focused on developing artificial intelligence methods for lead discovery and applying them to design drugs for the treatment of cancer, inflammation, and cardiovascular disease. I have a particular interest in developing molecular modulators for protein conformational changes and protein-protein interactions.

I obtained my B.S. in Pharmaceutical Engineering from NanjingTech University in 2022.
